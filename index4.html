What is the best shoes for Forwards

  1. Lightweight Design
    Guards often rely on speed and agility, so lightweight shoes help them move quickly.
  2. Good Ankle support
    While not as high-top as some forward shoes, guards still benefit from adequate ankle support to prevent injuries during quick lateral movements.
  3. Traction
    A rubber outsole with a good tread pattern ensures grip on the court, allowing for quick cuts and stops.
  4. Cushioning
    Responsive cushioning helps absorb impact and provides comfort during long games. Look for shoes with EVA or gel cushioning.
  5. Breathability
    Materials that allow for airflow help keep feet cool and dry during play.
  6. Lockdown Fit
    A secure fit is crucial for stability. Features like lacing systems, straps, or adaptive materials can help achieve this.
